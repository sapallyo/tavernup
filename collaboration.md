# TavernUp – Collaboration

## Principles

- Interfaces are the default. The question is not "what speaks for it"
  but "what speaks against it".
- Modularization and reduction of dependencies take highest priority.
  When in doubt, add the abstraction layer.
- Time pressure is not a valid argument for structural shortcuts.
  Speed of implementation (with AI assistance) removes the usual
  cost justification for cutting corners.
- YAGNI applies to features, not to structural quality.
- Architecture decisions are discussed before implementation,
  not justified afterward.
- Claude proposes, Jürgen decides. Pushback on architecture is expected
  and welcome.

## Improving the Collaboration

- Suggestions to improve our way of working are always welcome and valuable.
- Any such improvement must be persisted immediately — in this file
  and in Claude's memories. Insights that only live in chat are lost.
